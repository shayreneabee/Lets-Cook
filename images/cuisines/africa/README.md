# Africa Cuisine Photography

This folder contains country-specific recipe photography for the Africa Cuisine Hub.

## Structure

- `nigeria/`: jollof rice, egusi soup, suya skewers, moi moi
- `ghana/`: waakye, red red, kelewele, groundnut soup
- `senegal/`: thieboudienne, chicken yassa, maafe, pastels
- `ivory-coast/`: attieke with grilled fish, kedjenou, aloco, foutou with sauce graine
- `ethiopia/`: doro wat, misir wat, tibs, injera
- `kenya/`: nyama choma, sukuma wiki, beef pilau, mandazi
- `tanzania/`: mishkaki, coconut fish curry, chipsi mayai, vitumbua
- `south-africa/`: bobotie, bunny chow, chakalaka with pap, koeksisters
- `zimbabwe/`: sadza with beef stew, muriwo unedovi, peanut butter rice, mapopo candy
- `morocco/`: chicken tagine, vegetable couscous, harira, chermoula fish
- `egypt/`: koshari, ful medames, molokhia, basbousa
- `tunisia/`: brik, lablabi, lamb couscous, ojja with merguez

## Image Rules

- Each image is assigned to one named recipe.
- Do not reuse an image for an unrelated dish or country.
- Replacement photography must preserve the exact dish identity and country attribution.
- Use warm natural light, authentic presentation, and no watermarks or text overlays.
- Card assets use a 3:2 landscape crop and are optimized as progressive JPEG files.

The current assets were generated as country-specific food contact sheets and cropped into individual recipe images for this project.
