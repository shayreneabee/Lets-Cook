# Mississippi Photography Slots

These slots support the Mississippi Delta & Piney Woods Cuisine chapter.

## Style

- Documentary, warm, natural-light food photography
- Real Mississippi settings: church kitchens, gas-station hot boxes, fish fries, reunion tables, country stores, cast iron, foil pans, and paper-lined baskets
- Food must match the named dish
- Avoid polished generic restaurant stock, unrelated substitutions, watermarks, and artificial-looking people

## Recommended Size

- Card images: 1600 x 1200 pixels, landscape
- Hero images: 2200 x 1400 pixels, landscape
- JPG or WebP, optimized below 350 KB when practical
